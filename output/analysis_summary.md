# tokyo_data analysis summary

## Revised design

- Shared panel window: 2016-2023
- Extended sample: 12 countries, 91 observations
- Balanced sample: 8 countries, 64 observations
- Extended countries: Macao SAR, China, Malaysia, Hong Kong SAR, China, Indonesia, Thailand, Singapore, Lao PDR, Philippines, Timor-Leste, Viet Nam, Korea, Rep., Japan
- Balanced countries: Macao SAR, China, Malaysia, Hong Kong SAR, China, Indonesia, Thailand, Singapore, Lao PDR, Philippines
- Threshold regression was dropped because the annual panel is too short and unevenly observed for reliable threshold identification.

## Main findings

- Same-year FE education coefficient: -0.1209 (p = 0.0005)
- Lagged FE education coefficient, extended sample: -0.0668 (p = 0.1907)
- Lagged FE education coefficient, balanced sample: -0.0909 (p = 0.1759)
- Next-year growth education coefficient: 0.0855 (p = 0.2812)
- Median 2023 indices relative to 2016: productivity = 106.8, education share = 74.0, internet use = 160.5.

## Interpretation

- The strong negative same-year coefficient weakens once education expenditure is lagged.
- The balanced-sample sensitivity check preserves the sign but remains statistically inconclusive with HC3 standard errors.
- The data support a cautious framing around delayed or conditional returns to education investment, not a strong threshold claim.
